## Books (foundational — apply across many topics)
- **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** — Martin Kleppmann (the single best book for HLD)
- **System Design Interview Vol 1 & 2** — Alex Xu (interview-focused, practical)
- **Database Internals** — Alex Petrov (deep dive on storage engines)
- **Clean Code** — Robert C. Martin (LLD foundation)
- **Head First Design Patterns** — Freeman & Robson (design patterns, approachable)
